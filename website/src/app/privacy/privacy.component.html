1. Privacy

Morphic Privacy Policy

June 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Morphic. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

User Accounts: We collect your email address and an encrypted password when you create an account. This allows you to securely access your surveys and data.

Images: Morphic captures images from Google Images for survey and analysis purposes.

Survey Data: When you create and fill out surveys in Morphic, we store the survey questions, responses, and tags associated with each image.

Search Queries: We retain the search queries you input for gathering images from Google Images.

Session Data: We store session information in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in between visits.

3. How We Use the Information

User Accounts: To authenticate you and associate your surveys and data with your account.

Images: Used solely for the purpose of your surveys and analyses.

Survey Data: To help you analyze and answer ecological questions.

Search Queries: To automate image searches on your behalf.

Session Data: To maintain your login session and provide a seamless user experience.

We do not share, sell, lease, or rent any personal data collected to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Morphic takes the security of your data very seriously. While no online service is 100% secure, we work very hard to protect your information against unauthorized access or loss.

5. Data Retention

Data is kept indefinitely to ensure your surveys and research remain accessible. If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please send an email request to pierrekin@househugo.com.

6. Open Source Project

Morphic is an open-source project licensed under GPL v2.0. While the source code is accessible to all, data generated by users (like survey responses) remains private and is not part of the open-source offering.

7. External Links

Morphic may link to other websites, like Google Images or the Chrome Web Store, which have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy in response to changes in the law or our practices. We'll post any changes here, so revisit this page regularly.

9. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact:

  • For technical issues or data deletion requests: pierrekin@househugo.com
  • For research-related questions: gabi.leighton1@gmail.com

By using Morphic, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the tool.
